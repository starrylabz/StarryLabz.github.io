// PARASITE POOL

The pool we run on

Parasite is a decentralized solo Bitcoin mining pool — every block reward goes 100% to the miner who finds it.

🪲

parasite.space

Parasite is a solo mining pool created by Zach, built on a modified fork of ckpool. When you mine on Parasite, you compete for the full block reward — no pool fees taken from your cut, no shared payouts. Your miner, your block, your sat.

Visit parasite.space Connect Guide → GitHub →
How to connect
01Set your stratum URL to parasite.wtf:42069
02Set your username to your Bitcoin address (e.g. bc1q...)
03Set password to anything — x works fine
04Track your stats at parasite.space/user/YOUR_ADDRESS
Why Parasite

Solo mining means every hash counts toward your own block — no splitting rewards with thousands of other miners. Parasite makes solo mining accessible to small rigs and Bitaxe builders.

Open source, community-run, and built specifically for the solo miner who believes in decentralization.
